The problem we are solving

A breakdown in Uganda means more than a stuck car. It means not knowing what is wrong, who to call, how long help will take, or what a fair price is. MOTOFIX replaces that uncertainty with speed, clarity and trust — from the phone you already own.

Key functionalities

Everything a driver needs when the road gets rough — in one app.

🤖

AI Diagnosis (MOTOBOT)

Describe the problem or snap a photo and our AI tells you what is likely wrong, repair-or-replace, and a fair cost range.

📍

Smart Mechanic Matching

Instantly matched with the nearest verified mechanic or tow provider who can handle your specific problem.

🗺️

Real-Time Tracking

Watch your mechanic approach on a live map with an ETA that updates as they travel.

💳

Transparent Pricing

An up-front cost estimate and a clear quote before any work begins. Pay by Mobile Money or cash.

🔧

Spare Parts & Dealers

Find the genuine parts your fix needs, with price guidance and trusted dealers in one place.

🛡️

Insurance Marketplace

Compare cover from multiple insurers, apply for a policy and file claims — in a few taps.

⛽

Fuel Station Finder

Locate nearby fuel stations with directions when you are running low.

🔔

Maintenance Reminders

Smart, due-aware reminders that keep your vehicle road-ready and help avoid breakdowns.

How it works

From breakdown to back-on-the-road, in four steps.

1

Request help

Open the app and tell MOTOFIX what is wrong — type it, speak it, or snap a photo.

2

Get matched

The nearest verified mechanic or tow provider who fits your need is assigned in minutes.

3

Track them in

Follow the mechanic on a live map with a real-time ETA until they reach you.

4

Fix & pay fairly

See a clear price before the work, then pay by Mobile Money or cash. No surprises.

Research & field visits

We went to the streets and garages of Kampala to understand the problem first-hand.

Before writing a line of code, we spoke with the people MOTOFIX is built for — drivers and boda-boda riders about their roadside experiences, and both roadside and professional mechanics about how repairs really happen. These conversations shaped every feature in the platform.
